Message from Dean of Student Welfare

Dr. Urmi Thakkar

Dean of Student Welfare

Somaiya Vidyavihar University

At Somaiya Vidyavihar University, student welfare forms the cornerstone of our educational philosophy. Our unwavering commitment transcends academic excellence, encompassing the holistic development and well-being of every student.

The Earn-While-Learn Scheme exemplifies our dedication to ensuring that financial barriers never impede educational aspirations. This transformative initiative empowers students to acquire valuable professional experience while advancing their academic journey.

"Our mission is to cultivate an environment where every student can flourish, innovate, and emerge as tomorrow's changemakers."
